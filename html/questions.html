T levels are a level 3 qualification that are equivalent to 3 A levels. During your T level placement you will complete a minimum of 315 hours of work experience.

Using the Bee Network app, you can check which modes of transport you can use to travel to your placement.

If you are 16-18 years old and living in Greater Manchester, you are eligible for an OurPass which gives you unlimited travel on any bus service within greater manchester and 50% off on tram tickets.

Yes, you must do your full 315 hours of work experience to complete your T level qualification.

If you are going to be late, make sure you let your line manager know beforehand via their communication methods such as e-mail or Microsoft Teams.

Most employers dont immediately offer T level students an apprenticeship right after a placement. But it is worth asking your line manager about it
